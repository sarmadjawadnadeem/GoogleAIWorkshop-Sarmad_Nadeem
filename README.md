## ✨ What Is Improve Socially?

Improve Socially is a NotebookLM-inspired intelligence workspace built specifically for social media analysis.

Instead of uploading PDFs, users upload their **social media export archives** (ZIP files downloaded from Facebook, Instagram, X/Twitter, Snapchat, LinkedIn, TikTok, Reddit, YouTube, and more).

The platform then creates a powerful AI workspace where users can:

* Analyze years of social media history
* Discover behavioral patterns
* Understand personal branding strengths
* Identify reputation risks
* Improve employability
* Improve scholarship and admission profiles
* Evaluate social presence from multiple professional perspectives
* Generate growth recommendations
* Explore interactive intelligence maps
* Ask natural-language questions about their online history

All powered by **Gemini 2.5 Pro**.
